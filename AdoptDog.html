How to Adopt a Dog from A-Care

Step 1: Browse Available Dogs

Visit our website or come to our shelter to see the dogs available for adoption. Each dog has a profile with information about their age, breed, temperament, and any special needs.

Step 2: Submit an Application

If you find a dog that captures your heart, fill out an adoption application form online or in person. This form helps us understand your lifestyle and preferences to ensure a good match.

Step 3: Meet Your Match

Once your application is approved, we'll arrange a meet-and-greet with the dog. Spend time getting to know each other and see if you're the perfect match!

Step 4: Finalize the Adoption

If everything goes well during the meeting, you can finalize the adoption process. We'll complete the necessary paperwork and send you home with your new furry family member!
